

**OBS-Gofa Back Matter**

**Oosuwan shaaketa**

Nunni misiley de7iya ha guutha maxaafa dumma dumma doonan asa ubbaas gathanaw koyoos; neeni nuura oothanaw danda7aasa. Taybon guutha asay hessa oothanaw danda7okona. Shin Kiristoosa ammanida ubbay issife oothanaw banta kushiya immiko maxaafa birshanawunne asa ubbaas gathanaw danda7etees.

**Gatey baynna imma**

Ha maxaafa neeni koyida mela asas immanaw danda7aasa. Nuus teqey baynna ooso piqaadey de7iya gisho, dumma dumma doonan birshetida maxaafa interneete bolla demmanaw danda7etees. Hessas ay gateka cigganaw bessena. Gujada ha adraashan demmanaw danda7aasa.  [[http://openbiblestories.com/|http://openbiblestories.com]].

**Haratas aatha**

Ha teqey baynna taariketa biidotanne mobayle apilikeeshineta hara doonatan demmanaw danda7aasa [[http://openbiblestories.com/|http://openbiblestories.com]]. Neeni eriya hara doonan he taariketa birshanaw danda7aasa.
